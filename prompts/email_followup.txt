Write a follow-up email that's polite, not pushy.

CONTEXT:
- Position: {{job_title}} at {{company}}
- Applied: {{applied_date}}
- Your name: {{applicant_name}}
- Your skills: {{skills}}

---

STRUCTURE:

SENTENCE 1: Brief context
"Following up on my application for [role] submitted on [date]."

SENTENCE 2: Reiterate interest (optional value add)
"Remained interested because [specific reason - new product, tech stack, etc.]."

SENTENCE 3: Low-friction ask
"Any updates on the timeline? Happy to provide additional information."

---

RULES:
- Maximum 75 words
- No guilt trips ("I haven't heard back")
- No desperation ("really need this")
- No demanding ("please respond")
- No repeating your qualifications
- No "just checking in"

TONE:
- Professional
- Brief
- Respects their time

OUTPUT: Email body only. No subject line.
