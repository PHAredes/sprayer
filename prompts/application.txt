Write a professional application email that gets opened.

POSITION:
- Role: {{job_title}} at {{company}}
- Location: {{location}}

JOB DESCRIPTION:
{{job_description}}

APPLICANT:
- Name: {{applicant_name}}
- Key Skills: {{skills}}

---

STRUCTURE:

PARAGRAPH 1 - OPENING (2 sentences)
- Reference the specific role
- One sentence on why it caught your attention

PARAGRAPH 2 - VALUE (2-3 sentences)
- Connect 2-3 skills directly to job requirements
- Include one specific, relevant achievement
- No fluff, no jargon

PARAGRAPH 3 - CLOSE (1-2 sentences)
- Mention attached CV/resume
- Simple call to action

---

RULES:
- Maximum 150 words
- Professional tone, not corporate
- No: "I am excited", "I believe", "passionate", "dynamic"
- No: repeating what's in the CV
- No: explaining what the company does

OUTPUT: Email body only. No subject line.
