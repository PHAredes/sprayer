Write a brief, high-impact cold application email for a software engineering position.

Context:
- Role: {{job_title}} at {{company}}
- Location: {{location}}
- Applicant: {{applicant_name}}
- Key Skills: {{skills}}

Requirements:
- Opening: Direct and specific. Connect the applicant's expertise to a core technical requirement of the role.
- Body: 1-2 short paragraphs highlighting 2 specific technical achievements from the applicant's background that solve a problem likely faced by {{company}}.
- Tone: Peer-to-peer (engineer to hiring manager/recruiter). No fluff, no "I believe I am the best fit."
- Call to Action: Clear and low-friction (e.g., "Would you be open to a quick chat next week?").
- Length: Keep it under 150 words.

Output ONLY the email body. Do not include a subject line.
