CV RULES:

1. PROFESSIONAL SUMMARY (2-3 lines)
   - Lead with years of experience and primary expertise
   - Mention 2-3 technologies most relevant to THIS job
   - Skip generic "results-driven professional" language

2. SKILLS SECTION
   - Group: Languages, Frameworks, Tools, Platforms
   - List most relevant to this job FIRST
   - Remove skills not mentioned in job description

3. EXPERIENCE
   - Reorder to show most relevant positions first
   - Each bullet point starts with action verb
   - Include metrics: scale, performance, team size
   - Highlight projects using tech from job description

4. EDUCATION
   - Include if < 10 years experience OR specifically relevant
   - Otherwise: one line at bottom

5. FORMATTING
   - Clean, scannable structure
   - Maximum 1 page (4000 characters)
   - Use consistent date formatting
   - No pronouns ("I", "my")

ACTION VERBS:
- Built, Developed, Created, Designed, Implemented
- Led, Managed, Directed, Coordinated
- Reduced, Improved, Increased, Decreased
- Launched, Shipped, Delivered, Deployed

FORBIDDEN:
- "Responsible for"
- "Worked on"
- "Helped with"
- "Participated in"
- Any lying or exaggeration
- Information not provided above
